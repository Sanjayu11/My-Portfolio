My Achievements

BrandSkulpt Challenge @ IIT Delhi

Participated in the BrandSkulpt Challenge, a UI/UX design hackathon conducted as part of BECon'24 by IIT Delhi. The event focused on reimagining brand experiences and creating intuitive digital interfaces. Although we didn’t win a prize, it was an inspiring experience filled with creativity, learning, and collaboration with talented designers from across the country.

1st Prize - Bot Jungle 2024

We secured 1st place in a Robotics Workshop and Robo War competition conducted at the School of Engineering, CUSAT, organized by IEEE CUSAT SB. This event was a blend of hands-on learning and real-time robotic combat, which sharpened our technical skills and teamwork.
